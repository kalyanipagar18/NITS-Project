Canteen Facility

Overview

The MET BKC canteen offers a spacious and well-ventilated environment where students can enjoy a variety of hygienic and nutritious meals at subsidized rates. The food served has a homely touch, catering to diverse tastes while ensuring cleanliness and health standards.

Highlights

  • Spacious seating with natural ventilation
  • Affordable, oil-free meals
  • Hygienic preparation and service areas
  • Filtered drinking water
  • Snack and meal options available throughout the day
