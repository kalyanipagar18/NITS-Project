MET World of Music (MWM)

Overview

The MET World of Music (MWM) is a dedicated space where students nurture their musical talent and creativity. Equipped with a variety of instruments and sound systems, it serves as a platform for both beginners and experienced musicians to collaborate, practice, and perform.

Facilities & Features

  • Soundproof music practice rooms
  • Guitars, keyboards, drums, tabla, and more
  • Recording setup with mic and speakers
  • Regular music events and talent shows
  • Support for forming student music bands

Access & Participation

Open to all students during designated hours. Music faculty and student clubs manage activities, auditions, and events throughout the academic year.
