College Bus Service

Overview

MET BKC provides a safe and punctual bus transportation system covering major areas of Nashik city and nearby regions. Buses are equipped with GPS tracking, comfortable seating, and follow strict safety and hygiene protocols to ensure a smooth commute for students and faculty.

Key Features

  • Well-maintained and GPS-enabled buses
  • Fixed schedule aligned with class timings
  • Comfortable seating and clean interiors
  • Emergency first aid and fire extinguishers available
  • Separate routes for Nashik and nearby towns
