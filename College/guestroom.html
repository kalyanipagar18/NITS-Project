Guest Room Facility

Overview

MET BKC provides a well-furnished guest room facility for visiting faculty, parents, and dignitaries. These rooms are designed to offer a comfortable stay within the campus premises with all basic amenities and a peaceful environment.

Facilities

  • Air-conditioned rooms with attached washrooms
  • Wi-Fi connectivity and work desk
  • Clean linen and housekeeping
  • Dining arrangement on request
  • Close access to academic blocks and auditorium

Booking Information

Guest rooms can be booked in advance through the college administration office. Availability depends on prior reservations and institutional requirements.
