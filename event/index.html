About Us

MET Bhujbal Knowledge City is a center for academic and extracurricular excellence. Every year, we organize a wide range of technical, cultural, and sports events to foster creativity, leadership, and innovation among students. Our goal is to build a vibrant campus life and empower students to achieve holistic development through events like hackathons, cultural fests, alumni meets, and more.
