About Department

Prof. Samir S. Shaikh

Computer Coordinator

Department of Computer Science and Design Engineering was established in the year 2021 for regular shift with the intake of 60 students. The department is running the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere approved course in Computer Science and Design Engineering.


For the aspirants taking engineering education, it is very important to have abilities to apply knowledge of mathematics and sciences to engineering problems. It is also crucial to have good analytical and data interpretation skills so that one can identify the problems and provide optimum solutions. At the same time, they should have also understanding of perceptive of professional and ethical responsibilities.


Department has well-equipped laboratories, well-qualified and dedicated faculties along with Industry-exposure. All faculties are consistently promoting the study of latest technologies and inventions in the field of Computer Science and Design which boost the confidence of students.

Department shapes the overall development of students as well as computer professionals by acquainting them with Industrial Visits, Seminars and Workshops; as well as conducting co-curricular activities and allowing students to participate in various competitions organized by various Engineering colleges. For development of student’s personality as a basic need of professional, the various activities are conducted by resource persons. Apart from four-wall classroom teaching the students pursue industrial training and can also pursue various courses through Massive Online Learning Resources, such as SWAYAM-NPTEL.
