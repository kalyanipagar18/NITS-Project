OUR VISION & MISSION

Our Vision

To develop Institute as a Centre of Excellence for developing globally accepted professionals having application orientation, lifelong learning approach towards socio-economic growth of stake holders and the Society as a whole.

Our Mission

  • Preparing Graduates capable enough to practice effectively and responsibly to technological changes and pursue advanced engineering.
  • Inculcate applied research to support and contribute to the social and economic well-being of citizens and organizations in nearby region.
  • Cultivate the entrepreneurial spirit and linkage of engineering education and business for technology commercialization.
  • Evolve thoughtfully to the changing needs of industry and surrounding world.
