ACADEMIC DEVELOPMENT

Departmental Activities

At IOM, every department strives for the grooming of students and facilitating them with activities that would enrich the budding managers with knowledge and experience of the industry scenario and upgrade them with the essential qualities and know-how’s of the industry. For the same, Marketing, Finance & HR Meets are organized. In which the students get an opportunity to learn from the industry stalwarts and to understand the requirements and expectations of Industry. During these meets domain experts from Industry are called on campus where students get an opportunity to interact with them. The intension of these meets is to bridge the gap between the academics and industry leanings.

Engineer’s Day

“Managers dream about doing great things, Engineers do them.” Our students understand the value of every element of an organisation. All major businesses run because of the efforts of engineers and so to acknowledge the contribution of engineers, our production department celebrates engineer’s day. This is again another attempt to cultivate the innovative thinking ability in our students so as to develop new ideas for the betterment of their businesses.

Congruence

MET Congruence is meticulously designed by a special team comprising of students with an aim to attract participation of the brightest minds from the top B-schools of the country in this grand event. MET Congruence is spread over 3 days, culminating in a grand finale, at the MET campus. MET Congruence is the true personification of the spirit of togetherness and unity. The event gives students a chance to use their wit, knowledge and passion, to prove their mettle in varied aspects, like decision making, creativity, self–confidence, business acumen and leadership skills. One of the main aims of MET Congruence is to establish long lasting relationships, with the industry, and engage the students in discussions and debates on what unity can achieve and dissension cannot

Women’s Day

various workshops and seminars are organised department wise based on current industry scenario on topics like current trends in Banking, Derivatives – Futures & Options, Financial budget analysis, Labour laws, Industrial relations, Mathemetical models in Marketing, current trends in Marketing, Mergers & Acquisitions, Six Sigma, Etc.
